❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I file a complaint?

You can file a complaint in three ways:

  • Use the online form by clicking "File Complaint"
  • Use voice input through "Voice Complaint (AI)" feature
  • Contact our helpline or visit citizen service center
How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Resolution time depends on the category and priority:

  • Critical issues: 24-48 hours
  • High priority: 3-7 days
  • Medium priority: 7-15 days
  • Low priority: 15-30 days
How can I track my complaint?

You can track your complaint using either:

  • Your unique Complaint ID (e.g., JS-2026-123456)
  • Your registered phone number

Go to "Track Complaint" section and enter your details.

Is the AI voice complaint system multilingual?

Yes! Our AI assistant supports multiple Indian languages including:

  • Hindi (हिंदी)
  • Tamil (தமிழ்)
  • Bengali (বাংলা)
  • Telugu (తెలుగు)
  • Marathi (मराठी)
  • And more...
